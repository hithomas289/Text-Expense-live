Common Questions

Can multiple crew members send receipts to one account?

Yes. Anyone can text receipts to your TextExpense number. Great for foremen, crew leads, or purchasing managers.

How do we track different construction phases?

Add phase names as notes when sending receipts (e.g., "foundation", "framing", "finishing").

Can we separate material waste from usage?

Add notes about returns, waste, or damaged materials. Track actual vs purchased amounts for better future estimates.

Does it work for commercial construction?

Absolutely. Track large commercial projects, multi-unit developments, or residential - all the same easy process.

Can we track equipment across multiple job sites?

Yes. Note equipment numbers or IDs when sending purchase or rental receipts. Track assets across projects.
