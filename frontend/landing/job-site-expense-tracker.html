Common Questions

What if the job site has no cell signal?

Take photos as usual. WhatsApp will queue them and send automatically when you reconnect to signal.

Can we track expenses for temporary sites?

Yes. Use job or site names as notes. Temporary or permanent sites tracked the same way.

How do we separate personal vs site expenses?

Only send site-related receipts to TextExpense, or note "site - [location]" to categorize them.

Can the whole crew send receipts?

Absolutely. Any authorized person can text receipts to your number. Great for distributed teams.

Does it work for international job sites?

Yes. WhatsApp works globally. Send receipts in any currency from any location.
