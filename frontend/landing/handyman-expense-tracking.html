Common Questions

How do I track supplies bought for multiple clients in one trip?

Send one receipt, add notes listing all clients. Split costs manually when billing or exporting.

Can I mark up materials for clients?

Yes. Export shows actual costs. Add your markup when creating client invoices.

What if I forget which job a receipt was for?

Contact us via WhatsApp. We can help add client or job notes to receipts after sending.

Does it track mileage between jobs?

Send fuel receipts with odometer notes. Manual mileage tracking based on fuel fill-ups and locations.

Can I separate residential vs commercial jobs?

Yes. Add "residential" or "commercial" tags in notes when sending receipts.
