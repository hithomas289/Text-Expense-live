Common Questions

Should I use standard mileage or actual expense method?

Track both. Standard mileage is simpler, actual expenses may be better if you drive a lot. Choose at tax time.

How do I prove business mileage to the IRS?

Photo fuel receipts with odometer readings from online shifts. Shows business purpose and miles driven.

Can I deduct commercial rideshare insurance?

Yes. Rideshare insurance premium is fully deductible business expense. Save all policy receipts.

What about phone and data plan costs?

Deduct business percentage of phone costs. Track phone and accessory purchases as business expenses.

How do I calculate vehicle depreciation?

Track vehicle purchase price and business mileage percentage. Your accountant calculates depreciation deduction.
