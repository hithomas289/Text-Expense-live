Common Questions

Can I deduct my home office space?

Yes if you have dedicated space for work. Track portion of rent/mortgage, utilities, internet based on square footage.

What percentage of internet can I deduct?

Deduct the business-use percentage. Track total bills and note approximate business vs personal split.

Can I write off my home office desk and chair?

Yes. Home office furniture used exclusively for work is tax deductible. Track all purchases.

What about coworking space memberships?

Fully deductible as business workspace expense. Save all membership fees and day pass receipts.

Can remote employees deduct home office?

Rules vary. Track expenses anyway. Consult tax professional about your specific employment situation.
