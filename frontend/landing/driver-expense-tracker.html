Common Questions

Can I track standard mileage vs actual expenses?

Yes. Track both fuel costs and mileage with odometer notes. Choose whichever method gives better deduction at tax time.

How do I separate work miles from personal driving?

Only send fuel receipts from work shifts with odometer readings. Personal driving stays separate.

Can I track expenses for multiple gig platforms?

Absolutely. Tag receipts with platform name (Uber, Lyft, DoorDash). Track costs per platform.

What about rideshare accessories like phone mounts?

Photo any work-related purchase receipt. All driver business expenses are deductible.

Can I track shift-by-shift profitability?

Yes. Tag fuel by shift, compare costs to earnings from that shift for true profitability.
