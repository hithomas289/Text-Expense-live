Common Questions

Can I track expenses by sales territory?

Yes. Add territory names as notes when sending receipts to organize expenses by region.

How do I separate client entertainment from personal meals?

Only send business meals to TextExpense, or add client names to clearly mark business entertainment.

Can I track mileage for territory coverage?

Send fuel receipts with odometer readings as notes to manually track territory mileage over time.

What if I have receipts in different currencies from international sales?

TextExpense automatically detects and records currency from receipts. Perfect for global sales teams.

Can I show my manager deal-specific expenses?

Absolutely. Filter your Excel export by deal or prospect name to show all related costs.
