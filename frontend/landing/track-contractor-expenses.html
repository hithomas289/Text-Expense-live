Common Questions

How do I separate personal vs business expenses?

Only send business receipts to TextExpense, or add "business" notes to categorize them when sending.

Can my office manager send receipts too?

Yes. Anyone can send receipts to your TextExpense number. Great for assistants or team members.

What if I buy supplies for multiple jobs in one trip?

Send one receipt, add note listing all jobs. Or split costs manually when creating your report.

Does it track mileage for work vehicles?

Send fuel receipts and add odometer readings as notes to manually track mileage over time.

Can I access old receipts for warranty claims?

Absolutely. All receipts are stored and searchable. Find any purchase from any time period.
