Common Questions

What meal information does the IRS require?

Date, amount, restaurant, attendees, and business purpose. TextExpense captures first three automatically, you add attendees and purpose as notes.

Can I track 50% deductible vs 100% deductible meals?

Yes. Note meal type when sending receipts. Categorize for tax purposes when exporting.

How do I document who attended the meal?

Add client names or attendee list as notes when sending receipt. Required for IRS audit defense.

What about alcoholic beverages on meal receipts?

All receipt details captured. Your accountant can handle alcohol-specific tax treatment.

Can I track meals by sales stage (prospect vs client)?

Absolutely. Tag receipts as "prospect", "client", or "closing" for pipeline analysis.
