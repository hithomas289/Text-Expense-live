Common Questions

Can I track expenses for multiple jobs at once?

Yes. When sending receipts via WhatsApp, add the job name or client name as a note. Filter by job when downloading your Excel report.

What if my hands are dirty or I'm wearing gloves?

WhatsApp works with voice commands on most phones. Or take the photo quickly and text it when you're cleaned up - takes 10 seconds.

Can I separate materials from tools for taxes?

Yes. Add notes like "materials" or "tools" when sending receipts. Organize them into categories when creating your tax report.

Does it work for subcontractor payments?

Absolutely. Photo any receipt or invoice from subcontractors and send it via WhatsApp. Keep everything documented.

Can I show clients itemized material costs?

Yes. Download your Excel file filtered by that client's job. Shows every material purchase with dates, amounts, and receipt images.
