Common Questions

When should I start tracking creator expenses?

Immediately. Even if content is a hobby now, track expenses in case it becomes income later.

Can I deduct home studio space?

Yes if you have dedicated space for content creation. Track home office portion of rent, utilities.

What if I use equipment for personal and business?

Track the purchase and deduct business-use percentage. Note usage split for tax purposes.

Do I need an LLC to deduct expenses?

No. Sole proprietors deduct business expenses on Schedule C without forming an LLC.

Can I write off content creation courses and education?

Yes. Business education that improves skills or income is tax deductible.
