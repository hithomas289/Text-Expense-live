Common Questions

What business expenses are tax deductible?

Ordinary and necessary business expenses. Common categories: supplies, advertising, vehicle, meals, travel, utilities, rent.

Do I need to keep receipt images for IRS?

Yes. IRS requires substantiation of expenses. Receipt images provide required proof.

How long should I keep business receipts?

IRS recommends 3 years minimum. Keep 7 years for major purchases and assets.

Can I deduct home office expenses?

Yes if you have dedicated business space. Track office supplies, portion of utilities, internet, etc.

What if I get audited?

Complete receipt documentation with images and categorization. Shows IRS you maintain proper records.
