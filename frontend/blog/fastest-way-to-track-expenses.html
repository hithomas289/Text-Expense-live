How to Make It Even Faster

Track immediately - Don't save receipts for later. Handle them right when you get them at the store or restaurant.

Use simple categories - Don't overthink it. Food, transport, business, personal - keep categories broad and quick to select.

Don't batch process - Tracking 10 receipts at once seems efficient but takes longer than doing each immediately in 30 seconds.

Skip perfectionism - Close enough is fine. If you're not sure which category, pick one and move on. You can always adjust later.
