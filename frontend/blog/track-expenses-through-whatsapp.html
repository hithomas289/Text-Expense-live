"Do I need WhatsApp Business or regular WhatsApp?"

Regular WhatsApp works perfectly. You don't need WhatsApp Business. Just use the WhatsApp you already have on your phone.

"Can I track expenses for multiple businesses or projects?"

Yes. Add notes when texting receipts: "Project A," "Client XYZ," "Personal Business." Filter and export by project or client.
