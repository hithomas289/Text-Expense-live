+

Our AI reads the receipt image and extracts key information like merchant name, date, amount, and tax. You'll see what we found, confirm it's correct (or make edits), then choose a category. Everything gets saved and organized in your account.

Can I categorize my expenses however I want?

+

Yes. You can use our standard categories or create custom ones that match how you think about your spending. In your Excel reports, you can filter by any category to see specific types of expenses.

What if I need receipts from several months ago?

+

All your receipts stay saved. When you generate an Excel report, you choose the timeframe (1, 2, or 3 months). Each report includes links to download the original receipt images for your records.
